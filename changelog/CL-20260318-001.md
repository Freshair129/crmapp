## Summary
Refined the First-Expired, First-Out (FEFO) deduction logic in `completeSessionWithStockDeduction` to ensure a complete audit trail in the `StockDeductionLog` even when registered lots are insufficient.

## Changes
- **Audit Logging Refinement**: Updated `scheduleRepo.js` to calculate and log the "remainder" portion of a stock deduction when available lots do not cover the full quantity requested. This prevents gaps in the audit trail where master stock levels would decrease without a corresponding log entry.
- **Floating Point Precision**: Implemented `toFixed(6)` for stock calculations to avoid standard JavaScript precision issues during lot-by-lot deduction.
- **Traceability Enhancement**: Every entry in `StockDeductionLog` now either links to a specific `lotId` (Phase 20+) or correctly identifies a remainder deduction without a lot ID.

## Root Cause
The previous implementation of `completeSessionWithStockDeduction` only created log entries for quantities matched within registered `IngredientLot` records. If the required quantity exceeded total lot stock, the remaining amount was deducted from the master `Ingredient.currentStock` but never written to the audit log.
